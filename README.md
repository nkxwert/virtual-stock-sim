**📈 Virtual Stock Simulator **

Virtual Stock Simulator is a realistic and interactive stock market simulation platform where users can practice investing without risking real money 💸

Build your own virtual portfolio, trade stocks in a market-like environment, and test different investment strategies safely and easily. Whether you're a beginner learning the basics or an experienced trader experimenting with strategies, this simulator is designed for everyone 🚀

✨ Features 📊 Real-time or simulated stock market experience 💼 Virtual portfolio management 📈 Profit & loss tracking 🏆 Rankings and leaderboards ⚡ Simple and user-friendly trading interface 

Our goal is to make stock market learning more fun, accessible, and practical for everyone 🌍
